MRI_DistortionPhantom

Geometric distortion in Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) is a serious problem. The purpose of this repository is to provide code such that users can automatically build a simple and cheap MRI distortion phantom to characterise MRI distortion.

Compared to other MRI distortion phantoms, this is:

  • Free and open source

  • Completely user customizable

  • Water free: light weight and leak free

  • Designed for cheap and easy construction
